Attack on Titan

Shingeki no Kyojin

Synopsis

Set in a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction, walls protect the last remnants of human civilization from gigantic humanoid creatures known as Titans. The story follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they join the military to fight the Titans and uncover dark secrets about their world.

Main Characters

  • Eren Yeager

    Determined and often reckless, driven by a desire for freedom and vengeance against the Titans.

  • Mikasa Ackerman

    A highly skilled soldier and Eren's adoptive sister, fiercely protective and composed.

  • Armin Arlert

    Strategic thinker whose intelligence often turns the tide of battles.

Seasons

  • Season 1 — Introduction and fall of Wall Maria
  • Season 2 — Revelations about Titans
  • Season 3 — Political intrigue and truth behind the walls
  • Final Season — Escalation and a global perspective

Why it matters

Attack on Titan is notable for its bold storytelling, moral ambiguity, and evolving scope — shifting from a contained survival tale to a complex exploration of power, history, and identity.

Quick facts

  • Format: TV anime / Manga adaptation
  • Genres: Dark fantasy, action, mystery
  • Author: Hajime Isayama
  • Status: Completed
  • Age rating: TV-14

Suggestions

Start with Season 1, then follow the release order. The manga provides full context for the ending.
