Takopi's Original Sin

Takopii no Genzai

Synopsis

Takopi travels from his home of Happy Planet to spread happiness on Earth, but meets the unsmiling Shizuka. Her school friends and home life causing her somber expression, and Takopi is determined to change that! Watch a story of inherently happy creature meeting ugliest sides of our society, through interactions of the main characters of the story.

Main Characters

  • Takopi

    A small, round creature from Happy Planet, who is determined to spread happiness and smiles wherever he goes.

  • Shizuka Kuze

    Sad appearing girl, meets Takopi on her way back from school. Her school friends and home life causing hardships in her life.

  • Marina Kirarazaka

    Shown as popular schoolgirl, causing problems for Shizuka, not rarely going too far in her bullying.

  • Naoki Azuma

    Shizuka's classmate, like her and notices undeserving treatment, but too afraid to stand up for her due to problems at home.
